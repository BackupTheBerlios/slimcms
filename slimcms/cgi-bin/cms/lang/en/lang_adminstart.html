Main Data

Here you should provide a title, description and keywords in order to push your site in search engines. Moreover you can define a standard footer that is displayed on every page and give your name resp. the name of your company for the Meta Tags »Author«, »Publisher« and »Copyright«.

Blocks

The main part of slim-CMS. Define new blocks, upload already existing Content or delete no more used stuff.
To edit a block you just have to click on it. For editing content we use FCKEditor that works like a text processing application.

Menu

In »Menu« you define the navigation menu for your website.

Layout

Choose your favourite layout by selecting the radio button. Click on thumbnail to get a preview of the template. The site layout can be changed any time, no information will be lost. All blocks, content and definition of the navigation is kept.

Logo

Here you can upload your own Logo. Click on »Browse« to pick your logofile from the filesystem. It's only possible to upload GIF-Files. ATTENTION! Only one logo can exists for your website. If you upload a new logo the old one is overwritten.
